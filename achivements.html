Gym / Fitness Club of the Year

A flagship award recognizing overall excellence in facilities, member experience, programming, and community reputation. Examples include “Asia’s Best Gym Award” at Asia Awards and the “Big Award – Gym of the Year” in the UK.

Innovation / Best Use of Technology

Honors gyms that leverage cutting-edge fitness tech, programming, or unique service delivery. Asia’s Fitness/Gym category includes awards like “Fitness Innovator of the Year” and “Best Use of Social Media for Health & Fitness”. The BIG Awards similarly offer an “Innovation Award” for creative approaches.

Best Independent / Standalone Gym

Acknowledges top-performing non-franchise and boutique gyms. Categories from Asia’s awards include “Best Standalone Gym” and “Independent Gym of the Year”.

Community Impact / Active Workplace / Outdoor Gym

Granted to gyms that deeply engage with and contribute to local communities or environments. Asia’s awards include “Active Workplace of the Year,” “Family Friendly Gym,” and “Outdoor Health & Fitness Event”. The BIG Awards have a “Community Award” and “Outdoor Fitness Centre of the Year”.

Best Personal Trainer / Instructor Awards

Individual recognition for exceptional trainers. Categories across awards include “Personal Trainer of the Year (Gym-Based),” “Fitness Instructor of the Year,” and “Group Exercise Instructor of the Year” .

Best Fitness Gear / Equipment Supplier / Nutrition Brand

Acknowledges excellence in providing gyms with top fitness equipment, gear, or nutritional support. Asia’s Fitness Awards list “Fitness Gear of the Year,” “Equipment/tools supplier of the Year,” and “Nutrition Brand of the Year”.

Unleash Your Inner Athlete

Unleashing your inner athlete” is about adopting an athlete’s mindset—setting bold goals, embracing discipline, and pushing through mental and physical challenges. By visualizing success and using positive self-talk, you prime yourself to perform better and stay committed.You treat each workout as part of a larger journey—chipping away at growth through small, steady wins.It’s also about mental resilience—learning to stay focused under stress and viewing rest and recovery as key to overall progress . Ultimately, it transforms gym sessions into purposeful training and empowers you to become your strongest self.
